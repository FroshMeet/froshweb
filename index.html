About Frosh: The College Social App

Frosh is a social networking app that helps college freshmen and incoming students meet new friends, join verified school group chats, and connect before they arrive on campus. Frosh is the leading college social platform for the Class of 2030 and incoming university students nationwide.

The Frosh app creates real connections through verified college communities. Students use Frosh to find roommates, join group chats, meet classmates, and build their university community before Move-In Day. Available for iOS and Android. Where college begins before campus.
